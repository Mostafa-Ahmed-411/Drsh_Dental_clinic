Our Story

The Story Behind Drsh Clinic

Drsh Clinic was founded with a single mission: to bring world-class dental care to the people of Assiut. Dr. Mostafa Ahmed recognized that patients in Upper Egypt deserved access to the same premium dental treatments available in major cities — delivered with warmth, professionalism, and respect.

From day one, the clinic was built on three pillars: clinical excellence, modern technology, and genuine patient care. Every piece of equipment is carefully selected. Every treatment protocol follows the latest evidence-based guidelines.

Today, Drsh Clinic is one of the most trusted dental practices in Assiut — known for transformative smile makeovers, painless implant procedures, and orthodontic results that speak for themselves.

The Doctor

Dr. Mostafa Ahmed

Consultant in Cosmetic & General Dentistry

BDS | Diploma in Cosmetic Dentistry | Certified Implantologist

Dr. Mostafa Ahmed is a consultant in cosmetic and general dentistry with over six years of dedicated clinical practice. He specializes in modern dental treatments, smile makeovers, dental implants, orthodontics, and advanced restorative procedures — all while focusing on patient comfort and long-term results.

His approach to dentistry is deeply personal: he takes the time to understand each patient's unique needs, crafting individualized treatment plans that combine technical precision with a genuine desire to help people feel confident in their smiles.
